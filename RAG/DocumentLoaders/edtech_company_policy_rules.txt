10 Policy Rules for an EdTech Company

1. Student Data Privacy
All student and instructor data must be stored securely and handled in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Academic Integrity
Users must not engage in plagiarism, cheating, or any form of academic misconduct while using the platform.

3. Respectful Communication
All communication between students, instructors, and staff must remain professional and respectful.

4. Content Ownership
Educational materials uploaded by instructors remain their intellectual property unless otherwise agreed upon.

5. Platform Usage
Users must use the platform only for educational purposes and avoid any illegal or harmful activities.

6. Account Security
Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and account information.

7. Attendance and Participation
Students are expected to attend scheduled sessions regularly and participate actively in learning activities.

8. Payment and Refund Policy
Course fees must be paid on time, and refund requests will follow the company’s official refund guidelines.

9. Anti-Discrimination Policy
The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy for discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind.

10. Compliance with Terms
Violation of company policies may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent termination of access to the platform.
